Game

How to Play

Short version: choose a building, choose the input/outputs, and then place it in the world. Make sure the proper items are going in through the proper buildings. Use conveyor belts to get everything back to your base. The area will shrink after the round ends.

About

An entry for the 2020 Incremental Game Jam (https://incrementalgamejam.dsolver.ca/home). Created by a team of developers: Crimson406, eisbaerBorealis, amd Embi.

This is a very basic prototype.
